Hello!

Our Lady of Fátima High School is a Catholic school in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India, affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education board located at Ramghat Road, Aligarh. The principal is Sr. Jyotsana. Our Lady of Fatima was established in 1961, and was the first English medium school in Aligarh. It is managed by Catholic missionary nuns. The school runs a program for educating underprivileged children in the evenings.

  • School's New Building

    Our school has recently got a new building which is meant for classes 8th to 12th. Earlier our school only extended till class 10th but now we have classes till 12th.
